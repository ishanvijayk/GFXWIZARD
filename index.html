Privacy Policy for GFX Wizard

Last updated: 1/1/2025

Introduction

Welcome to GFX Wizard. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

GFX Wizard collects the following information:

  • App usage statistics to optimize performance
  • List of installed applications for optimization purposes
  • System performance metrics (CPU, RAM, Temperature)

Permissions We Request

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS: To monitor system performance
  • KILL_BACKGROUND_PROCESSES: To optimize system performance

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Optimize your device performance
  • Provide system statistics
  • Improve app functionality
  • Deliver personalized optimization recommendations

Advertising

We use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect and process data according to their privacy policy. For more information, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
